Discovering Alpha in Crypto Investments

In the current financial market dynamics, alpha is both mysterious and complex, making it inaccessible to most. Investors are vexed with two pressing concerns: 

  • being exposed to a vast amount of unintelligible information that must be considered efficiently for informed investment decisions and
  • unreliable sources of this information that instill fear of investment
Considerably, investors are left with no alternative but to choose a centrally managed investment firm to plan and manage their funds.

Acknowledging this, leveraging the power of crypto and its true financial inclusion requires decentralized solutions like non-custodial asset management firms and autonomous alpha discovery. This combined with Al, blockchain technology, and a dynamic web3 asset management tool that can provide predictive algorithms to decentralize the access to alpha opening the pathway for a truly democratized adoption

a_fi vaults are tokenized investment strategies designed to provide optimized returns. Tokenization helps to democratize access to investment strategies that were previously only available to institutional or high-net-worth investors. By creating a token that represents ownership in a particular investment strategy or portfolio, smaller investors can gain exposure to these strategies and potentially benefit from their returns. Several features are incorporated into the vaults. a_fi vaults have automated portfolio rebalancing that involves periodically reviewing the performance of the portfolio and making adjustments as needed as per a pre-coded logic.
